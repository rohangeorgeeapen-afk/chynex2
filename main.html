Hands are the most precious gift to a person. Imagine someone who has spent decades doing everything with their hands. Every tender gesture, every careful task, every creative expression depended on their hands. They learned to write with them. They learned to love and create with them.

Then in an instant, it's gone.

And what does the world offer to them? A mechanical device that makes noise and feels bulky. The experience is like using a typewriter after a lifetime with a laptop.

We believe it is time to offer something people can truly adapt to. Something that remembers what it means to be human.

There is a better way.

We are working to build bionics that feel so natural, you will forget you lost a body part. Our work is about creating things that do not exist yet, always searching for new solutions and learning from each step.

We are developing technology to make human limbs and even humanoids possible. The same vision guides both efforts. Movement should feel natural, intuitive, and alive.

Holding a loved one. Eating without help. Dressing yourself. Touching your face without thinking.

These experiences are not just special moments. This is life. It is an extension of you that never feels separate from who you are.

We want to give you back your life and we're working on it.

This is our promise.
